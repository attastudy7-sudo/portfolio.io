SESA - Student's Mental Assessment Tool

SESA was designed to help schools detect and respond to student wellbeing concerns earlier through structured assessments, smart risk signaling, and role-based visibility for counsellors and administrators. I built the full frontend experience and core backend workflows to ensure that screening, review, and follow-up actions felt simple and guided for non-technical staff.

I also focused heavily on trust and clarity in the interface by implementing secure student data handling patterns, clear status indicators, and clean dashboard summaries that made school-wide trends easy to interpret. The final product gave education teams a practical way to move from reactive support to consistent, policy-aligned intervention planning.

Knowly - Educational Resource Platform

Knowly was built as a student-first learning hub where users could discover quality revision material quickly without information overload. I crafted the search and filtering experience to support fast retrieval of notes, exam prep content, and grouped resources while keeping the layout lightweight on slower devices and school networks.

Beyond discovery, I implemented usability improvements such as clean card hierarchy, readable preview states, and mobile-friendly interaction patterns that reduced friction for day-to-day study sessions. The platform evolved into a more focused resource environment where students could spend less time searching and more time learning.

Booking Website for Henna Art

This booking website project centered on turning social-media traffic into confirmed client inquiries through a cleaner conversion journey. I designed and implemented structured service pages, testimonial emphasis blocks, and direct booking calls-to-action that guided visitors from browsing to action with minimal confusion.

Special attention was given to mobile layout quality, speed, and contact reliability because most customer traffic came from phones. By simplifying page flow, sharpening service storytelling, and improving inquiry touchpoints, the site became a stronger digital storefront that supported consistent bookings and improved trust with first-time visitors.
