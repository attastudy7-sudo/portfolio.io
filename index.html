SESA - Student's Mental Assessment Tool

SESA centralises student wellbeing data into guided assessments and role-specific dashboards so staff can spot risks, prioritise cases, and track interventions. I implemented compact, accessible UI components, secure data flows, cohort filters, risk scoring, and an action-tracking timeline, plus exportable reports and admin controls. The interface is optimised for low-bandwidth school networks and keyboard accessibility, reducing friction for non-technical users while supporting clear, auditable follow-up workflows.

Motown.AI — AI Study Platform

Motown.AI — AI Study Platform

Motown.AI turns lecture slides and PDFs into personalised study packs — flashcards, quizzes, fill-in exercises, AI notes, and a podcast — in minutes. Built for university students in Ghana (KNUST, UG, UCC), the platform features a dark-mode study room, spaced repetition progress tracking, quota-gated tiers, Paystack subscription payments, and a community Discover feed where users share generated packs. Engineered with Flask, Supabase Postgres, Cloudinary, and a multi-provider AI pipeline (Gemini + OpenRouter).
